Three Keystone Habits for 2017

Introduction

Hi, my name is Kenn. Towards the end of last year, I reached a not-too-pleasant life milestone. I was overweight and and just could not muster up the energy and motivation to do anything. I did however come across a book called "The Power Of Habit," and I realized two things:
  • Throughout my life I have been accumulating positive and negative habits, and
  • I can leverage positive habits while eliminating negative ones.
Below are three keystones habit I have been implementing into my life since mid-December 2016 and that have helped level-up my life.

Early Riser with a Morning Routine

Early Riser

By nature, I am a night owl. Although right now I am comfortably getting up at around 6:30 am, my goal is to eventually START my day by 6am. Here are my top 3 reasons why I want to become an early riser:

  1. Instead of being rushed to meet the needs of others, I now start each day foused on myself and my needs.
  2. There is something comforting and motivating about waking up with the sun rising.
  3. By the time other people are starting their day, I've already gotten a three hour head start on my day.
  • Bonus: I actually get intense focused work done because there is no distractions.

Morning Routine

And because by getting up early, I've incresed the number of hours in my day, I've also starting incorporating some pretty powerful morning routins; again all which I am able to get done before I start my day meeting other people's expections and deadlines. Here are my top 3 morning routines:

  1. Making my bed as soon as I get up
  2. Meditating for 15+ hours
  3. Training for my first marathon

Meditation

I used the HeadSpace App to keep me motivated and to provide me direction with my medittion practice. I prefer to meditate in the morning so that I can prime my day for gratitude and success. I find that by doing so, I my days are purposeful and I am not the stress-case that I used to be.
